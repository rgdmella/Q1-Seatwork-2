Character in Noli Me Tangere

Photo Name Description
Crisóstomo Ibarra He is wealthy man who studied in Europe for a few years and soon came back to the Philippines. He is also the son of Don Rafael Ibarra.
Don Rafael Ibarra The father of Crisóstomo Ibarra. He does unfair practices of the Spanish.
Captain Tiago He is a wealthy but racist man and makes sure to keep in touch with high hierachy. He is also the father of María Clara
Padre Dámaso Padre Dámaso is an old Spanish priest. He tries to bother those who have got in his way.
Padre Siblya He is a priest in Manila. He helps Padre Dámaso bother those at the return of the Ibarra's.
